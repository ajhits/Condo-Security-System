What we do

VigilanceAI offers a range of powerful features to enhance your home security and provide peace of mind. Here are some key features of VigilanceAI.

Intelligent Surveillance

VigilanceAI utilizes advanced video analytics to monitor security cameras and provide real-time alerts for suspicious activities or intrusions.

Facial Recognition

With facial recognition capabilities, VigilanceAI can identify familiar faces, allowing for personalized notifications and enhanced security.

Motion Detection

VigilanceAI employs motion sensors to detect any unauthorized movement within your home and immediately notifies you of potential threats.

Access Control

VigilanceAI integrates with smart locks, allowing you to remotely control and manage access to your home, granting temporary or permanent access to trusted individuals.

Smart Alerts

VigilanceAI sends instant notifications to your mobile device or computer whenever an unusual event or breach occurs, ensuring you stay informed in real-time.

Remote Control

With the VigilanceAI mobile app or web portal, you can remotely arm or disarm your security system, customize settings, and monitor your home's security status from anywhere in the world.
